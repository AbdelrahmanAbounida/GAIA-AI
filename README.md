## ğŸŒ What is GAIA AI?

**GAIA AI** is a powerful and flexible **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) framework** for building intelligent AI agents.

It enables you to combine:

- Structured & unstructured knowledge
- External tools and APIs
- MCP-based integrations
- Local or hosted AI models

All in one **modular, developer-friendly platform**.

Whether you're building **customer support bots**, **research assistants**, or **enterprise knowledge systems**, GAIA AI gives you full control.
